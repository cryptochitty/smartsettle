SmartSettle is an AI-powered autonomous payment agent that helps users save money and time on recurring bills. Users simply upload their invoice, and the agent automatically reads the bill, negotiates discounts with service providers, and pays the lowest agreed amount using stablecoins.

The system uses artificial intelligence to understand bill details, communicate with provider systems, and make smart financial decisions. Once the best offer is identified, the agent securely executes the payment on-chain and stores a tamper-proof receipt on the blockchain.

Built on Celo, SmartSettle enables fast, low-cost, and transparent digital payments designed for real-world users. The platform reduces late fees, removes manual payment hassles, and gives individuals the negotiation power usually available only to large customers.

SmartSettle brings together AI automation, blockchain trust, and stablecoin payments to make everyday bill management smarter, cheaper, and fully autonomous.


Our mission is to make everyday financial management effortless, affordable, and intelligent by building autonomous AI agents that act in the best interest of users.

SmartSettle empowers individuals with automated negotiation power, ensuring they never overpay bills or miss due dates. By combining artificial intelligence with secure on-chain payments, we aim to reduce financial stress, eliminate hidden costs, and bring transparency to recurring expenses.

Built on Celo, our goal is to deliver mobile-first, low-cost, and trustworthy financial automation that works for everyone — especially users who lack time, tools, or bargaining power.

We envision a future where AI agents handle routine financial tasks autonomously, allowing people to save money, save time, and focus on what truly matters.
